Our Story

Caterdaay was founded in 2016 in Toronto, Canada, and is the world’s largest marketplace for event catering.We make it simple to order food online for your office, parties, get-togethers and more. Caterdaay connects people with a network of reliable local caterers and restaurants across Canada. And we’re not just an elegant website. We feature a team of A-grade customer support professionals who are ready to help whenever and wherever. Caterdaay began with a handful of restaurants in Toronto. As our service matured, we expanded across Canada. Today, Caterdaay offers a curated list of the best business caterers for hundreds of cities and towns across Canada.
 

Catering for All

Whether you are looking for a caterer for a client meeting, wedding or birthday party, Caterdaay makes it effortless to order online from a vast catalogue of caterers accompanied by A-grade customer service.
 

Save Time and Make It Simple

Caterdaay ensures that you will never again need to hassle contacting restaurants and asking if they can deliver to your location. Instead, you can save valuable time and immediately identify caterers who not only serve to your address, but meet your food type, budget, dietary requirements and more. Best part of it all is that you can create, cancel or modify your orders anytime at all.
